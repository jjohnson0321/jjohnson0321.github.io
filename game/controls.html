Lancer

Regular Attack: Poke

Lancer's regular attack (left click) is a ranged poke that does a significantly more damage than other regular attacks to make up for its short range. This attack can only be pointed directly in one of the 4 cardinal directions.

Special Move: Spin Block

Lancer's special move (right click) is a spin-to-win attack that blocks incoming projectiles and does massive damage to all enemies in range.

Black Mage

Regular Attack: Fireball

Black Mage's regular attack (left click) is a simple fireball. This is a long ranged ability, but he can only create 2 fireballs at a time so watch out!

Special Move: Flame Wave

Black Mage's special move (right click) is a cone of fire that can be directed in any direction. Beware to enemies that get caught in this wave, they are in for a ton of damage.

Ninja

Regular Attack: Throwing Star

Ninja's regular attack (left click) is a short ranged projectile that can be shot in any direction. It is possible to run out of throwing stars (but they will return to your inventory after some time) so be sure to pick them up while you have the chance

Special Move: Slash Parry

Ninja's special move (right click) allows him to parry incoming projectiles from any of the four cardinal directions or do damage to enemies hit with his blade. Remember, timing is key to a successful parry.
